Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF file secure?
Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
What's the maximum file size?
You can compress PDF files up to 50MB in size for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process.
How much can I reduce file size?
Depending on your settings, you can typically reduce file size by 15-55% while maintaining quality. Results vary based on content type.
Does compression affect quality?
Our smart compression preserves text quality while optimizing images based on your selected settings. You control the quality vs size trade-off.
Can I compress multiple files?
Currently, you can compress one file at a time. Simply reset the tool after each compression to process another file.
Is there a usage limit?
No! You can compress unlimited PDF files completely free. No registration, no limits, no restrictions.
