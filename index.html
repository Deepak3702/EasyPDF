Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We believe in transparency. Here are answers to some common questions about EasyPDF.

    Yes, absolutely. All 29 tools on EasyPDF are completely free to use without any restrictions or watermarks. Our goal is to provide accessible tools for everyone.

    Your security is our top priority. Unlike other sites, EasyPDF uses client-side technology. This means your files are processed locally on your own computer. They are never sent over the internet or stored on our servers, guaranteeing your privacy.

A Deep Dive into the EasyPDF Suite

The true power of EasyPDF lies in its comprehensive and fully functional toolkit. Let's explore the key tools, grouped by their primary function.

Group 1: Document Management and Organization

  • Merge PDF: Combine multiple PDF files into a single, cohesive document. Perfect for compiling a report from different sources.
  • Split PDF: Extract specific pages or page ranges from a large PDF. Share only what's necessary.
  • Organize PDF: A powerful visual tool to re-order and delete pages by simply dragging and dropping thumbnails.
  • Rotate PDF: Easily fix pages that are scanned in the wrong orientation with a single click.
  • Add Page Numbers: Professionalize long documents by automatically inserting page numbers.

Group 2: The Conversion Powerhouse

  • Office to PDF: Convert your Word, PowerPoint, and Excel files into universal, professional-looking PDFs.
  • PDF to JPG / GIF: Convert each page of your PDF into high-quality images or an animated GIF. The new PDF to GIF tool is perfect for creating shareable content for social media.
  • Images to PDF: Compile one or more JPG or PNG images into a single, easy-to-share PDF document.

Group 3: Advanced Editing and Security

  • Sign PDF: Electronically sign documents by drawing your signature directly in the browser.
  • Protect & Unlock PDF: Add a password to secure your files, or remove a password if you have the key.
  • OCR PDF: A game-changing feature that recognizes text in scanned documents, making them searchable and selectable.
